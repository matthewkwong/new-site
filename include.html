Include

Director of Design

September 2019 - Current

Background

For the 2019 - 2020 school year, I am the Director of Design for Include, a non-profit organization at the University of California Davis that helps build websites for local businesses in the area.

Summary

As a director, I’ve learned and improved many skills such as leadership, teamwork, communication, organization, and decision making.

My responsiblities include: leading the design team, creating lesson plans, creating advertisements, and leading lessons on wireframing and UI/UX.

At the start of the 2019 school year, I created and lead workshops on wireframing and UI/UX. This was a chance for me to improve my skills as an instructor and leader as it was the first time many students had used some of the design tools.

As a club that builds websites for other non-profit organizations, I’ve met with clients and learned to communicate my ideas in a professional matter. After meeting with our clients, I supervised our design team on concept creation, strategy and user experience.
